About

I am a third-year UG student with Honor Computer Science and Applied Mathematics major at the University of Rochester. My research interests lie broadly in Artificial Intelligence and Data Mining. Recently, I am working on the Natural Language Processing (NLP) tasks including coreference resolution and information extraction, but my more specific interests within these areas are diverse. Some recent projects include:

  1. Develop a Deep Learning Model to extract the document level entity relation information for the Coreference Resolution downstream task with Aaron White's group as part of MagaAttitude in FACTS.lab.
  2. Build Instagram E-cigarette image and interpretation dataset and apply CNN (Convolution Neural Network), LDA (Latent Dirichlet allocation), Vadar for image information identification and Instagram post topic and sentiment analysis with Dr. Dongmei Li's Lab at URMC.
  3. Conducted research of translation between scRNA and scATAC data via Variational Autoencoders (VAE) and identify the association between transcriptomic and epigenomic data, which could be used to infer the new regulatory relations and present adequate downstream analyses which show the validity of the translated products in CIS Neoscholar Education Group.
Through collaborations, I have also engaged in work that is perhaps more within the domain of AI proper.
